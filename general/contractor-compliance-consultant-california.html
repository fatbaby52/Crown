Contractor Compliance Consultant for California Businesses

Running a contracting business in California means navigating a maze of regulations, certifications, and client requirements. Most small contractors don't have in-house compliance staff—but they still face the same requirements as larger competitors.

Crown Consulting provides the compliance expertise California contractors need, without the overhead of full-time staff.

What We Do

Crown Consulting helps small to mid-sized California contractors with three core areas:

Certifications. SBE, DVBE, DBE, MBE, WBE—we handle the applications, documentation, and renewals so you can access contracts that require certified small businesses.

Compliance. Cal/OSHA requirements, utility-specific standards, IIPPs, safety programs, employment law compliance—we build programs that satisfy regulators and clients.

Bidding. Government contracts, utility work, competitive proposals—we help you find opportunities, evaluate them, and submit winning bids.

Who We Help

We primarily work with:

Vegetation management contractors. Tree care, line clearance, wildfire prevention, fuels management. We understand utility vegetation management from the inside.

Utility contractors. Companies working for PG&E, SCE, SDG&E, and municipal utilities. We know what utilities expect from their contractors.

Small construction contractors. Family-owned businesses that need compliance support but can't justify full-time staff.

Subcontractors. Companies working under larger primes who need to meet flow-down compliance requirements.

Our sweet spot is the small to mid-sized contractor—big enough to need real compliance programs, but not big enough to have dedicated compliance teams.

Common Engagements

Contractors typically come to us for:

Certification projects. You need DBE certification to bid on a specific opportunity. You want DVBE status to access state contract preferences. We handle the application from start to finish.

Compliance program development. Your utility client is requiring an upgraded safety program. Cal/OSHA cited you for IIPP deficiencies. We build or rebuild your compliance programs.

Bid support. A significant opportunity is out for bid and you want help developing a competitive proposal. We help you analyze the scope, build pricing, and craft your response.

Ongoing compliance management. You need someone tracking regulatory changes, maintaining certifications, and keeping your programs current. We provide ongoing support on retainer.

Our Approach

Assessment. We start by understanding your business, your current situation, and your goals. What certifications do you need? Where are your compliance gaps? What opportunities are you pursuing?

Recommendations. We provide clear recommendations on priorities and approach. Not everything needs to happen at once—we help you focus on what matters most.

Execution. We do the work. Applications get filed. Programs get written. Bids get submitted. We're not just advisors—we're hands-on.

Transfer. Our goal is to build your capability, not create dependency. We create systems you can maintain and train your team to use them.
